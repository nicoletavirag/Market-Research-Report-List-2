<p><p>Polymerase chain reaction (PCR) market is segmented into three main types: Real-Time PCR, Traditional PCR, and Digital PCR. Real-Time PCR allows for the continuous monitoring of the amplification of a targeted DNA molecule during the PCR process. Traditional PCR is the standard method of PCR where the DNA amplification occurs in cycles. Digital PCR is a more precise method that quantifies the amount of DNA in a sample by partitioning it into thousands of individual reactions. Each type serves different purposes in genetic analysis.</p></p>
