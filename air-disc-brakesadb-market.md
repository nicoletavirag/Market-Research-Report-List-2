<p><p>Air Disc Brakes (ADB) market can be categorized into two types: Front Type and Rear Type. Front Type ADB are installed in vehicles where the front brakes handle the majority of the braking force. These brakes are crucial for stopping power and control. On the other hand, Rear Type ADB are designed to handle the braking force at the rear wheels, providing additional stability and control during braking. Both Front Type and Rear Type ADB are essential components in ensuring safe and efficient braking systems in vehicles.</p></p>
