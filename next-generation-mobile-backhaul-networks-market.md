<p><strong>The Next-generation Mobile Backhaul Networks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired Backhaul</li><li>Wireless Backhaul</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Next-generation mobile backhaul networks consist of wired backhaul and wireless backhaul markets. Wired backhaul involves the use of physical fiber optic cables or DSL lines to connect mobile base stations to the core network, providing high bandwidth and low latency. Wireless backhaul, on the other hand, uses microwave or millimeter wave links to transmit data between base stations and the core network, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness in areas where laying cables is not feasible. Both markets play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable mobile connectivity.</p></p>
