<p><p>Audio amplifiers market is segmented into different types based on their operating class. Class A amplifiers operate with the highest linearity and lowest distortion but are inefficient. Class B amplifiers are more efficient but have higher distortion. Class AB amplifiers strike a balance between efficiency and distortion. Class D amplifiers are highly efficient but may have higher switching distortion. Other amplifier types may include Class G, Class H, and Class T, each offering unique features and trade-offs in performance and efficiency.</p></p>
