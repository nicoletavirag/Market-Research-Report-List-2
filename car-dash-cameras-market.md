<p><p>Car dash cameras come in two main types: single lens and dual lens. Single lens cameras have one camera lens and capture footage of the road ahead. Dual lens cameras, on the other hand, have two camera lenses that can capture footage of both the road ahead and the interior of the car. Single lens cameras are more common and cost-effective, while dual lens cameras provide additional security by recording both the road and inside the vehicle simultaneously.</p></p>
