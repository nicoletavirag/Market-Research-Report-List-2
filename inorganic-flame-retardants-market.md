The market types include Aluminum Trihydrate (ATH), which releases water vapor when heated, Magnesium Hydroxide (MDH), which decomposes at high temperatures to release water, Antimony Trioxide (ATO), which acts as a synergist with other flame retardants, Zinc Borate (ZB), which releases water vapor and carbon dioxide when heated, and others. These inorganic flame retardants play a crucial role in enhancing the fire safety of various products.</p></p>
