<p><p>Polyurethane resin market can be categorized into three main types - solvent based polyurethane resins, water based polyurethane resins, and other market. On the other hand, water based polyurethane resins are more eco-friendly and have lower VOC emissions. The "other market" category includes unique polyurethane resin products that do not fall under the solvent or water based categories, such as powder coatings or UV-curable resins.</p></p>
