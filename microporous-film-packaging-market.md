<p><ul><li>Mechanical Perforation</li><li>Laser Perforation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microporous film packaging utilizes different methods for creating perforations to control the airflow and moisture in the packaging. Mechanical perforation involves physically puncturing the film with needles or blades to create holes, while laser perforation uses a laser to create precise perforations without damaging the surrounding area. Both methods are used in various industries such as food packaging and healthcare to ensure the quality and freshness of the products inside the packaging.</p></p>
