<p><strong>The Polyaspartate Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Coatings</li><li>Water-Based Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyaspartate coatings can be classified into two main types based on the solvent used in the formulation: solvent-based coatings and water-based coatings. Solvent-based coatings typically use organic solvents such as acetone or toluene to dissolve the polyaspartate resin before application. On the other hand, water-based coatings use water as the main solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and safer to use. Both types of coatings offer excellent protection and durability, with water-based coatings gaining popularity due to their lower VOC emissions and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
