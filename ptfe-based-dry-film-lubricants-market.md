<p><p>PTFE-based dry film lubricants are available in three main types: liquid, spray, and others. Liquid PTFE-based lubricants come in a liquid form that can be applied directly to the surface using a brush or dropper. Spray lubricants are aerosol cans that allow for easy and convenient application. Other types of PTFE-based lubricants include wipes, gels, and powders. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of application method and suitability for different surfaces or equipment.</p></p>
