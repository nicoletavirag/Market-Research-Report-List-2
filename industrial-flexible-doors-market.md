<p><p>Industrial flexible doors come in three main types: Fixed, Hinged, and Sliding. Fixed doors are immobile and cannot be opened or closed. Hinged doors swing open and closed on hinges, making them ideal for areas with limited space. Sliding doors move horizontally to open and close, saving space and offering easy access to larger openings. Each type of door has its advantages and is used in different industrial settings based on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
