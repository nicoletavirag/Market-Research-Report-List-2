<p><strong>The Airborne Radiometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Millimeter Wave Radiometer</li><li>Interferometric Correlator Radiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airborne Radiometers are categorized into two main types: Millimeter Wave Radiometer and Interferometric Correlator Radiometer. Millimeter Wave Radiometers operate at high frequencies and are used for atmospheric profiling and weather forecasting applications. Interferometric Correlator Radiometers use interferometry techniques to measure the phase difference between signals received by multiple antennas, enabling high-resolution imaging capabilities. Both types of radiometers are widely used in remote sensing applications for monitoring land, atmosphere, and oceans from airborne platforms.</p></p>
