<p><strong>The Bulk Explosives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (Powder)</li><li>ANFO</li><li>Emulsion Explosive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk explosives can be categorized into three main types: Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (Powder), ANFO, and Emulsion Explosive. Ammonium Nitrate Explosives consist of a mixture of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, which is commonly used in mining operations. ANFO, or Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil, is a mixture of ammonium nitrate and diesel fuel, which is widely used in construction and quarrying. Emulsion Explosives are water-based explosives that are stable and safe to handle, making them suitable for various civil engineering applications.</p></p>
