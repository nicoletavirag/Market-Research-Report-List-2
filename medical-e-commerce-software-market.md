<p><p>Medical E-commerce software can be categorized into web-based and electronic data interchange (EDI) markets. Web-based software allows for online ordering, billing, and patient management through a user-friendly interface accessible via a web browser. On the other hand, EDI software enables the electronic exchange of business documents such as orders and invoices between healthcare providers and suppliers. These two types of markets cater to different needs within the healthcare industry, providing convenience and efficiency in managing transactions and data exchange.</p></p>
