<p><p>Wet glass fiber nonwovens are categorized into two main types: long glass fiber and short glass fiber. Long glass fiber nonwovens have longer fibers that provide higher strength and durability, making them ideal for applications where structural integrity is important. On the other hand, short glass fiber nonwovens have shorter fibers that offer better flexibility and conformability, making them suitable for more intricate and detailed applications. Both types of glass fiber nonwovens are commonly used in industries such as automotive, construction, and filtration.</p></p>
