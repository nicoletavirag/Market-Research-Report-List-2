<p><p>Self-compacting concrete (SCC) is a type of concrete designed to flow and fill formwork without the need for vibration. There are three main types of SCC in the market: powder type, viscosity agent type, and combination type. Powder type SCC relies on the addition of powder to improve flowability. Viscosity agent type SCC uses specific chemicals to control the viscosity of the concrete. Combination type SCC incorporates both powder and viscosity agents to achieve optimal flow properties.</p></p>
