<p><p>Sealant web films are categorized based on their thickness, with four main market types being <15 μm, 15-35 μm, 35-50 μm, and >50 μm. Films that are <15 μm are extremely thin and are commonly used for packaging applications that require a lightweight and flexible material. Films in the 15-35 μm range are versatile and suitable for a wide range of packaging purposes. Films in the 35-50 μm category offer enhanced durability and barrier properties, making them ideal for protective packaging. Films >50 μm are thick and sturdy, often used for heavy-duty applications that require extra strength and protection.</p></p>
