<p><p>The Automotive IoT Modules Market is categorized into different types based on packaging including LGA Package, mPCIe Package, and Others. LGA Package modules are compact and soldered directly onto a PCB, offering space-saving benefits. mPCIe Package modules are designed to be plugged into a mini PCIe slot on a PCB, allowing for easy integration and replacement. The "Others" category includes modules with different packaging options, such as SIP or BGA, catering to diverse requirements in the automotive IoT sector.</p></p>
