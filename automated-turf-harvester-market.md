<p><p>Automated turf harvesters come in two main types: roll turf harvesters and slab turf harvesters. Roll turf harvesters are designed to cut and roll up long strips of turf, making them ideal for large-scale operations. Slab turf harvesters, on the other hand, are used to cut turf into individual sections, making them better suited for smaller areas or precision turf cutting. Both types of automated turf harvesters are efficient and effective tools for quickly and easily harvesting turf for a variety of landscaping projects.</p></p>
