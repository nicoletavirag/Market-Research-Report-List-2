<p><p>Broadband attenuators are available in two main types: manual and electric. Manual attenuators require physical adjustment by the user to control signal strength, making them more suitable for applications where precise control is desired. Electric attenuators, on the other hand, can be controlled remotely or automatically, offering greater convenience and flexibility in adjusting signal attenuation. Both types cater to different market segments based on their respective advantages in terms of control and ease of use.</p></p>
