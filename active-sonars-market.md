<p><strong>The Active Sonars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Beam Sonar System</li><li>Side Scan Sonar System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Sonar systems can be divided into two main types: Multi-Beam Sonar Systems and Side Scan Sonar Systems. </p><p>Multi-Beam Sonar Systems use multiple beams of sound to provide a wide coverage area and high-resolution images of the seafloor. These systems are commonly used for mapping and surveying purposes.</p><p>Side Scan Sonar Systems, on the other hand, use a single beam of sound that is swept across the seafloor to create detailed images of underwater terrain, objects, and structures. This type of sonar is often used for search and rescue operations and underwater archaeology.</p></p>
