<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Medical CT Scan System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Common CT</li><li>Spiral Scan CT</li><li>Electronic Beam Tomography</li><li>Spectrum CT</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Common CT, also known as conventional CT, is the basic type of CT scan system that uses multiple X-ray images to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body. Spiral Scan CT, on the other hand, continuously rotates around the body to create a more detailed and faster scan. Electronic Beam Tomography focuses on specific areas of the body, providing high-resolution images. Spectrum CT captures images at different energy levels. Other types include Dual-source CT, Cone Beam CT, and Photon-counting CT, each offering unique capabilities for medical imaging.</p></p>
