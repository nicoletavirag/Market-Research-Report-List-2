<p><ul><li>Ceramic Oxide Fiber</li><li>Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic Fiber Fabric Market includes two main types: Ceramic Oxide Fiber and Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber. Ceramic Oxide Fiber is made from oxides like alumina and silica, offering high temperature resistance and excellent insulation properties. On the other hand, Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber is made from non-oxide materials such as carbides and nitrides, providing superior strength and toughness. Both types of ceramic fiber fabrics are widely used in various industries for applications requiring high heat resistance, insulation, and mechanical strength.</p></p>
