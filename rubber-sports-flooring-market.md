<p><p>Rubber sports flooring can be categorized into two main types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber is derived from the sap of rubber trees and is known for its elasticity and durability. Synthetic rubber, on the other hand, is man-made using a combination of chemicals and materials to replicate the properties of natural rubber. Both types of rubber offer excellent shock absorption, slip resistance, and comfort, making them popular choices for sports flooring applications.</p></p>
