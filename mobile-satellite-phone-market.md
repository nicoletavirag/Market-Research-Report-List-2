<p><ul><li>Geosynchronous Satellite Telephone</li><li>Low-Earth Orbit Satellite Telephone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile satellite phone market includes two main types: Geosynchronous Satellite Telephone and Low-Earth Orbit Satellite Telephone. Geosynchronous satellites are positioned in fixed locations high above the equator, providing coverage over a large area but with a slight delay in signal transmission. On the other hand, Low-Earth Orbit satellites orbit close to the Earth's surface, offering lower latency and better signal strength but requiring a larger number of satellites for continuous coverage. Both markets cater to users needing reliable mobile communication in remote or disaster-prone areas.</p></p>
