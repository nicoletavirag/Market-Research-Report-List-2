<p><strong>The Tuned Passive Harmonic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Tuned Filters</li><li>Double Tuned Filters</li><li>Triple Tuned Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tuned Passive Harmonic Filters are classified into three types based on the number of tuned stages they contain. Single tuned filters have one tuned stage, double tuned filters have two tuned stages, and triple tuned filters have three tuned stages. The number of tuned stages determines the frequency range over which harmonics can be mitigated effectively. Single tuned filters are suitable for basic harmonic suppression, while double and triple tuned filters are used for more complex harmonic distortion issues requiring precise filtering at multiple frequencies.</p></p>
