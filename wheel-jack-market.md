<p><p>Wheel jacks are commonly categorized into three main types: hydraulic wheel jacks, pneumatic wheel jacks, and electric wheel jacks. Hydraulic wheel jacks use hydraulic pressure to lift vehicles, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Pneumatic wheel jacks rely on air pressure for lifting vehicles, making them ideal for quick and efficient lifting. Electric wheel jacks utilize electric motors for lifting, providing ease of use and convenience. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, offering flexibility and efficiency in vehicle maintenance and repair tasks.</p></p>
