<p><p>Biogas liquefaction market comprises two main types of technologies: Cryogenic Technology and Conventional Upgrading Technology. Cryogenic Technology involves the cooling and compression of biogas to turn it into a liquid form for easier transportation and storage. On the other hand, Conventional Upgrading Technology involves the removal of impurities and processing of biogas to increase its methane content before liquefaction. Both technologies play a crucial role in the biogas market by enabling efficient management and utilization of renewable energy sources.</p></p>
