<p><p>The 3D depth sensor market can be classified into three main types: Infrared, Laser, and Others. Infrared sensors use infrared light to determine depth and distance, making them suitable for indoor environments. Laser sensors emit laser beams and measure the time it takes for the beam to bounce back, providing precise depth perception for outdoor use. Other types of sensors, such as ultrasound or structured light sensors, offer alternative methods of capturing 3D depth data for various applications.</p></p>
