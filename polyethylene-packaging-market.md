<p><ul><li>High Density Polystyrene (HDPE)</li><li>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>Locked Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyethylene packaging market consists of three main types: High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Locked Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE is known for its high strength and chemical resistance, making it suitable for packaging chemicals and detergents. LDPE is flexible and lightweight, commonly used for packaging food and pharmaceuticals. LLDPE is a blend of LDPE and linear polyethylene, offering high puncture resistance and flexibility, often used for packaging materials like stretch wrap and liners.</p></p>
