<p><p>Fiber drop cables are divided into two main market types: single mode cable and multi mode cable. Single mode cable is designed for long-distance communication and has a smaller core size, allowing for a single pathway of light to travel through. Multi mode cable, on the other hand, has a larger core size and is typically used for shorter distances. It allows multiple pathways of light to travel through, making it ideal for high-speed data transmission over shorter distances.</p></p>
