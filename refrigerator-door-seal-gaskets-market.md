<p><p>Refrigerator door seal gaskets are available in two main types: rubber sleeve and magnetic strip. Rubber sleeve gaskets provide a flexible seal around the refrigerator door, effectively keeping cold air in and warm air out. On the other hand, magnetic strip gaskets use magnets to create a tight seal when the door is closed, preventing any air leakage. Both types of gaskets are essential for maintaining the efficiency of the refrigerator and keeping food fresh for longer periods.</p></p>
