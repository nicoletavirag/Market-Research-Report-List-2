<p><ul><li>Granular Resin</li><li>Flaky Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phenolic adhesives are divided into two main types: Granular Resin and Flaky Resin. Granular resin is in the form of small granules or beads that can be melted and applied to surfaces for bonding. On the other hand, flaky resin comes in the form of thin flakes or sheets that can be activated by heat and pressure to create strong bonds. Both types of phenolic adhesives offer durable and versatile bonding solutions for a variety of applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and aerospace.</p></p>
