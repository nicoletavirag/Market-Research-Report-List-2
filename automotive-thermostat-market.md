<p><ul><li>Insert Thermostat</li><li>Housing Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An insert thermostat is a type of automotive thermostat that is designed to fit into a pre-existing housing in a vehicle's cooling system. On the other hand, a housing thermostat is a complete unit that includes both the thermostat and the housing in a single assembly. Insert thermostats are often used for simpler installations or replacements, while housing thermostats are preferred for more complex cooling systems. Both types play a crucial role in regulating the engine's operating temperature and preventing overheating.</p></p>
