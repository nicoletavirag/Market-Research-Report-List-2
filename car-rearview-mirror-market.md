<p><p>Car rearview mirrors come in three main types - exterior mirrors, interior mirrors, and under rearview mirrors. Exterior mirrors are located on the sides of the car and provide visibility to the driver of surrounding vehicles. Interior mirrors are located inside the car and offer a view of the rear of the vehicle. Under rearview mirrors are located underneath the car and are used to monitor the road directly behind the vehicle. Each type of rearview mirror serves a specific purpose in providing the driver with essential visibility while driving.</p></p>
