<p><ul><li>Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Olive Oil</li><li>Olive Pomace Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic olive oil market consists of three main types: Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Olive Oil, and Olive Pomace Oil. Extra Virgin Olive Oil is the highest quality and purest form of olive oil, produced by cold pressing olives without any chemicals or heat. Olive Oil is a blend of refined olive oil and virgin olive oil, offering a milder flavor. Olive Pomace Oil is extracted from the residue left after the initial pressing, making it the least expensive but also the lowest quality option in the market.</p></p>
