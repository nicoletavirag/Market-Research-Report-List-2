<p><p>Potentiometer position sensors are divided into two main types - rotary and linear. Rotary potentiometer position sensors measure the angular position of a shaft, while linear potentiometer position sensors measure the linear movement of an object. These sensors are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing to provide precise and accurate position feedback. Rotary sensors are ideal for applications where rotational movement needs to be monitored, while linear sensors are suitable for applications requiring linear displacement measurement.</p></p>
