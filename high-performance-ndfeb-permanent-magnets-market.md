<p><strong>The High-performance NdFeB Permanent Magnets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded NdFeB Magnets</li><li>Sintered NdFeB Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-performance NdFeB Permanent Magnets are divided into two main types: Bonded NdFeB Magnets and Sintered NdFeB Magnets. Bonded NdFeB Magnets are made by mixing NdFeB powder with a binder and then pressing it into the desired shape. They are ideal for complex shapes and small volume production. Sintered NdFeB Magnets, on the other hand, are made by compacting fine NdFeB powder in a magnetic field and then sintering the material. They offer higher magnetic properties and are suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>
