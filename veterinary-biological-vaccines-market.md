<p><p>The Veterinary Biological Vaccines market can be categorized into Live Attenuated Vaccines, Inactivated Vaccines, and Others. Live Attenuated Vaccines are made from weakened forms of the virus or bacteria, providing a strong and long-lasting immune response. Inactivated Vaccines are made from killed pathogens, stimulating a weaker immune response but are safer for use in immunocompromised animals. The "Others" category includes subunit vaccines, toxoid vaccines, and DNA vaccines that offer unique approaches to immunization in veterinary medicine.</p></p>
