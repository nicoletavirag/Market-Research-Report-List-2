<p><p>Cotton yarn market types include carded yarn, combed yarn, and others. Carded yarn is made from fibers that are carded before being spun into yarn, resulting in a slightly rough texture. Combed yarn is made from fibers that have undergone an additional combing process, resulting in a smoother and more uniform yarn. Other types of cotton yarn may include mercerized yarn, which has been treated to improve its luster and strength, and blended yarn, which is made from a combination of different fibers.</p></p>
