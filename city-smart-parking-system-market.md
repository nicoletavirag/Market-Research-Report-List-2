<p><p>City smart parking systems can be categorized into two main types: on-street and off-street markets. On-street parking systems involve sensors and cameras installed on public roads to monitor and manage parking spaces in real-time. Off-street parking systems, on the other hand, are implemented in parking garages or lots, using similar technology to track available spots and guide drivers to open spaces. Both types aim to optimize parking efficiency, reduce traffic congestion, and improve the overall urban mobility experience.</p></p>
