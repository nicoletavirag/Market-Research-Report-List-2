<p><p>Elevator modernization market types include hydraulic elevator, traction elevator, climbing elevator, and pneumatic elevator. Hydraulic elevators use fluid power to move the elevator car, while traction elevators use ropes and counterweights. Climbing elevators are designed for steep inclines or vertical climbs, and pneumatic elevators use air pressure to move the car. Each type of elevator modernization offers unique benefits and features, catering to different building structures and requirements.</p></p>
