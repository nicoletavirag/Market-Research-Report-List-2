<p><p>Rotational magnetic speed sensors can be classified into non-contact and contact types based on their detection method. Non-contact sensors use magnetic fields to measure the speed of rotating objects without physical contact, making them ideal for applications where precision and durability are crucial. On the other hand, contact sensors require direct contact with the rotating surface to measure speed accurately. Each type has its unique advantages and is used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
