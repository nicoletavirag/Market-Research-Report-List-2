<p><strong>The Polyurethane Amine Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane amine catalyst market can be classified into two main types: reactive amine catalysts and non-reactive amine catalysts. Reactive amine catalysts are chemical compounds that actively participate in the polyurethane reaction, helping to speed up the curing process and improve the final properties of the material. Non-reactive amine catalysts, on the other hand, do not participate in the reaction but instead act as stabilizers or promoters, enhancing the effectiveness of other catalysts in the formulation. Both types play important roles in the production of polyurethane materials.</p></p>
