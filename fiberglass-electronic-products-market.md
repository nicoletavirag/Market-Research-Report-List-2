<p><p>The fiberglass electronic products market can be categorized into two types: long fiber and short fiber. These products are commonly used in applications requiring durability and reliability. On the other hand, short fiber products are made using chopped fiberglass strands, offering improved flexibility and molding capabilities. These products are commonly used in applications where intricate shapes and designs are required.</p></p>
