<p><ul><li>Organic Polysilazanes (OPSZ)</li><li>Perhydropolysilazanes (PHPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polysilazane market can be divided into two types: Organic Polyisilazanes (OPSZ) and Perhydropolysilazanes (PHPS). OPSZ consist of organic side groups attached to the silicon and nitrogen backbone, while PHPS are hydrogen-rich polysilazanes. OPSZ are commonly used in coatings, adhesives, and sealants, while PHPS are utilized as precursors for silicon nitride and silicon carbide ceramics. Both types of polysilazanes offer unique properties and applications in the chemical and material industries.</p></p>
