<p><p>Windscreen wiper blades are essential components of a car that ensure clear visibility during rainy or snowy conditions. There are three main types of wiper blades available in the market: boneless, bone, and hybrid. Boneless wiper blades are made of a single piece of rubber, bone wiper blades have a metal or plastic frame supporting the rubber blade, and hybrid wiper blades combine the features of both boneless and bone wipers. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of flexibility, durability, and performance.</p></p>
