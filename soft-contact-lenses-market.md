<p><strong>The Soft Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicone Hydrogel</li><li>Hydrogels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soft contact lenses market can be broadly categorized into silicone hydrogel and hydrogels. Silicone hydrogel lenses are made of a combination of silicone and hydrogel materials, allowing for more oxygen to reach the eye, making them more breathable and comfortable for extended wear. Hydrogel lenses, on the other hand, are made of a water-absorbing material that provides moisture to the eye throughout the day. Both types offer different benefits and options for individuals based on their needs and preferences.</p></p>
