<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray foam equipment market includes two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell spray foam equipment is used for applications requiring high insulation values and vapor retardation. It has a denser structure, making it ideal for outdoor use and areas prone to moisture. On the other hand, open cell spray foam equipment is used for interior applications and offers excellent sound absorption and flexibility. It has a softer structure, allowing for expansion and contraction without compromising the insulation properties.</p></p>
