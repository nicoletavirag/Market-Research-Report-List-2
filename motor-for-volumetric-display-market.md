<p><p>Digital Light Processing (DLP) Technology market for volumetric displays involves the use of tiny mirrors to reflect light and create an image. These mirrors tilt rapidly to create a high-resolution and high-brightness display. On the other hand, Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS) Technology market utilizes liquid crystal materials on a reflective silicon surface to generate images. This technology offers high resolution and contrast ratios, making it ideal for applications requiring detailed visual representation in volumetric displays.</p></p>
