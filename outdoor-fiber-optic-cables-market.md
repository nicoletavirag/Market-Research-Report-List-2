<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Outdoor Fiber Optic Cables Market is categorized into two main types: Single-Mode Fiber Optic Cables and Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cables. Single-Mode Fiber Optic Cables are designed for long-distance communication and have a smaller core size, allowing light to travel straight down the fiber without multiple paths. On the other hand, Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cables are suitable for short distances and have a larger core size, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through the fiber simultaneously. Both types play crucial roles in outdoor network infrastructure.</p></p>
