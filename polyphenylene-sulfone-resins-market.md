<p><p>Polyphenylene Sulfone Resins (PPSU) are available in two main types: Powder PPSU and Granular PPSU. Powder PPSU is finely ground PPSU resin that is commonly used in additive manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Granular PPSU is larger, pellet-like material that is typically used in injection molding applications. Both types offer excellent heat resistance, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties, making them popular choices in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and medical.</p></p>
