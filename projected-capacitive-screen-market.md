<p><strong>The Projected Capacitive Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Capacitance</li><li>Mutual Capacitance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Projected Capacitive Screen technology is segmented into two main types - Self Capacitance and Mutual Capacitance. Self Capacitance screens detect touch through changes in electrical charge on the display, making them well-suited for smaller devices. On the other hand, Mutual Capacitance screens use a grid of X and Y electrodes to detect touch, enabling more precise touch detection and multi-touch capabilities, making them ideal for larger displays such as tablets and smartphones. Both types cater to different market applications based on their unique capabilities.</p></p>
