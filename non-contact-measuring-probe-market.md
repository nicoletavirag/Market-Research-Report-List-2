<p><p>The Non-Contact Measuring Probe market includes two main types: Machine Tool Probe and CMM Probe. Machine Tool Probes are used in machinery to automate tool setting, workpiece set-up, and in-process inspection. They are crucial for improving accuracy and efficiency in manufacturing processes. CMM Probes are used on Coordinate Measuring Machines to measure the physical geometrical characteristics of an object. They play a vital role in quality control and inspection processes. Both types of probes are essential tools for precision measurement in various industries.</p></p>
