<p><p>Wearable antennas are divided into different market types based on the frequency band they operate in - L-band, S-band, C-band, and X-band. L-band antennas operate in the 1-2 GHz range, suitable for applications that require longer communication range. S-band antennas work in the 2-4 GHz range, ideal for satellite communication and radar systems. C-band antennas operate in the 4-8 GHz range, commonly used for satellite communication. X-band antennas operate in the 8-12 GHz range, known for high data rate communication and military applications.</p></p>
