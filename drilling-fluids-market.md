<p><strong>The Drilling Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-based Fluids (OBF)</li><li>Water-based Fluids (WBF)</li><li>Synthetic-based Fluids (SBF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drilling fluids market is broadly categorized into three types- Oil-based Fluids (OBF), Water-based Fluids (WBF), and Synthetic-based Fluids (SBF). OBF is made from petroleum products, providing better lubrication and temperature resistance. WBF is a cost-effective option made from water and various additives, suitable for most drilling operations. SBF is a more environmentally friendly option made from synthetic materials, offering improved performance and compatibility with sensitive formations. Each type of drilling fluid has its own advantages and applications in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
