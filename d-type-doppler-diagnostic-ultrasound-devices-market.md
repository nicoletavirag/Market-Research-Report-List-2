<p><p>The D type Doppler Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market includes Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed Wave (PW) devices. CW Doppler ultrasound uses continuous waves to measure blood flow velocity, typically for high-velocity flow. On the other hand, PW Doppler ultrasound uses short pulses of sound to measure blood flow at a specific location, providing details about both velocity and direction. Both types of devices play a crucial role in diagnosing conditions such as heart disease, vascular disorders, and monitoring fetal health during pregnancy.</p></p>
