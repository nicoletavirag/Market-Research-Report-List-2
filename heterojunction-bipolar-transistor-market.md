<p><p>Heterojunction Bipolar Transistors are classified into different types based on the materials used in their structure. NPN Heterojunction Bipolar Transistors have an N-doped layer sandwiched between P-doped layers. SiGe Heterojunction Bipolar Transistors use silicon-germanium layers for improved performance. InGaP Heterojunction Bipolar Transistors utilize indium gallium phosphide for enhanced efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of speed, power consumption, and frequency response, catering to diverse applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
