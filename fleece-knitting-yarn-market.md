<p><p>Fleece knitting yarn market is generally categorized into three types based on the fineness of the wool fibers: coarse wools, medium wools, and fine wools. Coarse wools, such as Lincoln and Romney, have thicker fibers and are ideal for heavier garments and outerwear. Medium wools, like Merino and Corriedale, have medium fineness and are versatile for a wide range of projects. Fine wools, including Cashmere and Alpaca, have the finest fibers and are prized for their softness and luxury, making them perfect for delicate knitwear and accessories.</p></p>
