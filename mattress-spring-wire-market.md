<p><strong>The Mattress Spring Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Coils</li><li>Bonnell Coils</li><li>Offset Coils</li><li>Marshall Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mattress spring wire market types include continuous coils, Bonnell coils, offset coils, and Marshall coils. Continuous coils consist of rows of coils connected by one continuous wire, providing consistent support. Bonnell coils are hourglass-shaped and connected with spiral wires for durability. Offset coils have a hinged design that allows for better motion isolation. Marshall coils are individually wrapped coils that respond independently to movement, reducing motion transfer. Each type of coil offers different benefits in terms of support, durability, and motion isolation.</p></p>
