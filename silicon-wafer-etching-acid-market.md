<p><p>The Silicon Wafer Etching Acid market can be divided into three main types - Hydrofluoric Acid, Nitric Acid, and Mixed Acid. Hydrofluoric Acid is commonly used for etching silicon wafers due to its high reactivity with silicon. Nitric Acid is also used for etching, but it is less commonly used compared to Hydrofluoric Acid. Mixed Acid, a combination of Hydrofluoric Acid and Nitric Acid, is another option for silicon wafer etching, offering a different etching profile compared to single acid etchants.</p></p>
