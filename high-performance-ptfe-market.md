<p><p>High Performance PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) is available in three main types: Granular, Aqueous Dispersion, and Micronized. Granular PTFE is in powder form and is commonly used in applications where a dry lubricant is needed. Aqueous Dispersion PTFE is in liquid form and is often used in coatings and lubricants. Micronized PTFE is a fine powder with particles of a specific size, providing improved performance in applications such as sealants and plastics. Each type of PTFE offers unique properties and benefits to meet different industry requirements.</p></p>
