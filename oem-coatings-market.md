<p><ul><li>Powder Coatings</li><li>Water-borne Coatings</li><li>Solvent-borne Coatings</li><li>Radiation Curable Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OEM Coatings are coatings applied by manufacturers to their products during the production process. The market for OEM coatings can be categorized into four main types: Powder Coatings, Water-borne Coatings, Solvent-borne Coatings, and Radiation Curable Coatings. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then cured with heat, while water-borne coatings use water as a solvent. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents, and radiation curable coatings are cured using UV or electron beam radiation. Each type of coating offers different advantages and is used for specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
