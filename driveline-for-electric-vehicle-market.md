<p><p>Series driveline in electric vehicles involves a single electric motor and a gearbox that transfers power to the wheels. Parallel driveline includes multiple motors with individual connections to the wheels. Power split driveline uses a combination of series and parallel configurations. Electric driveline market encompasses all driveline types for electric vehicles, with series and parallel being the most common. Each driveline type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, performance, and overall driving experience in the emerging electric vehicle market.</p></p>
