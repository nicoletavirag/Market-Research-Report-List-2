<p><p>Wax Anti-Settling Additives (WASA) are used in various industries to prevent settling of solid particles in wax-based formulations. There are two main types of WASA market: Solvent Based and Water Based. Solvent Based WASA are additives that are dissolved in solvents and are typically used in solvent-based coatings, inks, and adhesives. Water Based WASA are additives that are dispersed in water and are used in water-based formulations such as paints and coatings. Both types of additives help improve the stability and performance of wax-based products.</p></p>
