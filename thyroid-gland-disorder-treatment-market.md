<p><p>Thyroid Gland Disorder Treatment Market can be categorized into three main types: Hyperthyroidism, Hypothyroidism, and Others. Hyperthyroidism involves an overactive thyroid gland, which can be treated through medication, radioactive iodine therapy, or surgery. Hypothyroidism, on the other hand, refers to an underactive thyroid gland and is typically managed through hormone replacement therapy. The "Others" category includes less common thyroid disorders such as thyroid nodules and thyroid cancer, which may require surgical intervention, radiation therapy, or medication.</p></p>
