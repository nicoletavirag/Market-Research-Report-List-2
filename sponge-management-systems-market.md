<p><p>Sponge management systems market includes two main types of technology: Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) and Bar Codes. RFID systems use electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track sponges, providing real-time data on their location and usage. Bar code systems require scanning a printed label to record information about the sponge. Both technologies improve efficiency in healthcare settings by reducing errors and ensuring accurate inventory management. The market for these systems is expected to grow as healthcare facilities prioritize patient safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
