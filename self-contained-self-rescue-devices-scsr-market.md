There are two main types of SCSR devices - Compressed Oxygen SCSR and Chemical Oxygen SCSR. Compressed Oxygen SCSRs use pressurized oxygen cylinders to supply breathable air, while Chemical Oxygen SCSRs utilize a chemical reaction to generate oxygen. Both types of SCSR devices play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of workers in potentially dangerous settings.</p></p>
