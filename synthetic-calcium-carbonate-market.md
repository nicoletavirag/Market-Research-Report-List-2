<p><p>Synthetic Calcium Carbonate market is divided into two main types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is produced by crushing and grinding natural calcium carbonate, while PCC is synthetically produced through a chemical precipitation process. GCC is used in industries like paper, plastics, and paints, while PCC is commonly used in industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and personal care products. Both types of calcium carbonate are valuable additives due to their ability to improve product properties and performance.</p></p>
