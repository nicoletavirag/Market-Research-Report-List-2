<p><p>Braided ground jumpers come in two main market types: Non-insulated and Insulated. Non-insulated jumpers are simple braided wires that provide a direct electrical connection for grounding purposes, without any additional insulating material. On the other hand, insulated jumpers have a covering of insulation material, such as PVC or rubber, to provide protection against environmental factors and potential electrical hazards. Both types of jumpers are essential in various industrial applications for ensuring proper grounding and electrical safety.</p></p>
