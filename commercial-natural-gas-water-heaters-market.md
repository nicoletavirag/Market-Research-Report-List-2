<p><p>Commercial natural gas water heaters are available in two main types: tankless natural gas water heaters and tank natural gas water heaters. Tankless natural gas water heaters heat water on demand, providing hot water instantly without the need for a storage tank. On the other hand, tank natural gas water heaters store hot water in a tank for later use. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various commercial applications based on the specific requirements of the users.</p></p>
