<p><p>Water based packaging adhesives are categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Acrylic adhesives offer high bond strength and chemical resistance. Epoxy adhesives are known for their durability and impact resistance. Polyurethane adhesives provide flexibility and moisture resistance. Silicone adhesives offer heat resistance and UV stability. Other types of water based adhesives include ethylene vinyl acetate (EVA) and polyvinyl acetate (PVA) adhesives. Each type has unique characteristics that make them suitable for different packaging applications.</p></p>
