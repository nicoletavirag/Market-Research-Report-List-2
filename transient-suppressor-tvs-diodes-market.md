<p><p>Transient Suppressor TVS Diodes are commonly categorized into two main types: Uni-Polar and Bi-Polar TVS. Uni-Polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against voltage spikes in one direction only, making them suitable for applications where polarity is constant. On the other hand, Bi-Polar TVS diodes can handle voltage spikes in both directions, providing bidirectional protection. These two types cater to different market needs and are utilized in various industries for safeguarding electronic components against transient voltage events.</p></p>
