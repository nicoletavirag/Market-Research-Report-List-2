Course Computer is the brain of the autopilot system, processing information and giving instructions to the drive mechanism which controls the steering of the vessel. The Control Head allows the user to input commands and make adjustments to the autopilot system. Other components in the market include sensors, rudder feedback units, and hydraulic pumps that contribute to the overall efficiency and functionality of the autopilot system.</p></p>
