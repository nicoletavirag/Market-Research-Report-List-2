<p><p>Chip-on-board (CoB) LED market offers various types such as Lateral Chip, Vertical Chip, and Flip Chip configurations. In the Lateral Chip design, the LED chips are mounted horizontally on the PCB. In the Vertical Chip design, the LED chips are mounted vertically on the PCB. In the Flip Chip design, the LED chips are flipped upside down and directly mounted onto the PCB. These different configurations provide flexibility in designing LED lighting products with specific performance characteristics and form factors.</p></p>
