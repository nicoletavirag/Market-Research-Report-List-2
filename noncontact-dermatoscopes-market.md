Digital dermatoscopes, on the other hand, employ advanced technology to capture high-resolution images of skin lesions for digital analysis and storage. They often feature polarized light, adjustable magnification, and connectivity options for sharing images with colleagues or patients. Both types of dermatoscopes play a crucial role in the early detection and diagnosis of skin cancer and other dermatological conditions.</p></p>
