<p><p>Landing gear control systems market includes various types such as brake metering valves, parking brake valves, actuators, and others. Brake metering valves regulate the amount of brake pressure applied to the wheels during landing. Parking brake valves are used to engage and disengage the parking brakes. Actuators are responsible for moving and controlling the landing gear. Other types of landing gear control systems may include sensors, electronic control units, and hydraulic systems that assist in the safe operation and maintenance of aircraft landing gear.</p></p>
