<p><ul><li>Automotive Flat Tappets</li><li>Automotive Roller Tappets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Valve Lifter Market includes two main types: Automotive Flat Tappets and Automotive Roller Tappets. Automotive Flat Tappets are traditional valve lifters that have a flat surface on the bottom, which directly contacts the camshaft lobe. On the other hand, Automotive Roller Tappets have a roller on the bottom, allowing for smoother operation and reduced friction. Both types play a crucial role in the proper functioning of the engine's valve system, ensuring smooth and efficient operation.</p></p>
