<p><p>The Automotive Catalytic Converter Device Market consists of various types including Three-way Catalysts (TWC), Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC), Lean NOx Trap (LNT), Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR), and Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC). TWCs are used to reduce harmful emissions. LNCs reduce nitrogen oxide emissions in lean-burn engines. LNTs trap nitrogen oxides when the engine is running lean. SCRs use a reductant to convert nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water. DOCs are used in diesel engines to convert carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons into carbon dioxide and water.</p></p>
