<p><p>Multi-tariff energy meters come in two main market types: single phase and three phase. Single phase meters are typically used in residential settings where energy consumption is lower. Three phase meters, on the other hand, are more commonly used in commercial and industrial settings where higher levels of energy consumption are seen. Both types of meters allow for different tariff rates to be applied based on the time of day, allowing consumers to take advantage of off-peak pricing and potentially reduce their overall energy costs.</p></p>
