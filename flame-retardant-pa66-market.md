<p><p>Flame retardant PA66 market types can be categorized into Halogen Type and Halogen Free Type. On the other hand, Halogen Free Type flame retardants do not contain halogen elements and are considered more eco-friendly as they do not release harmful gases when burned. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on their properties.</p></p>
