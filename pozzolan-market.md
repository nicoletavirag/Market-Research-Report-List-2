<p><p>Pozzolan market can be divided into two main types: artificial pozzolan and natural pozzolan. Artificial pozzolan is produced through industrial processes, such as fly ash or silica fume. It is used as a cement replacement to improve strength and durability of concrete. On the other hand, natural pozzolan is a naturally occurring material, like volcanic ash or calcined clay. It also serves as a cement substitute in construction projects, enhancing the chemical properties of concrete. Both types of pozzolan play a crucial role in the construction industry.</p></p>
