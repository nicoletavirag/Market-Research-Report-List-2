<p><p>Bias tire market includes two main types: General Bias Tire and Bias Belted Tire. General bias tires are traditional, economical tires made with overlapping ply layers. These tires are typically used for off-road vehicles and agricultural machinery, offering durability and traction. On the other hand, Bias Belted Tires have an additional layer of belts under the tread for improved stability and handling at higher speeds. They are commonly found on motorcycles and some passenger vehicles. Both types of bias tires cater to different needs in the market.</p></p>
