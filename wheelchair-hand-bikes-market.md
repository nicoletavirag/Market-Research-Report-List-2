<p><p>Wheelchair hand bikes are divided into three main market types - manual, electric, and hybrid. Manual hand bikes are propelled by the user's own arm strength, providing a good workout and improving upper body strength. Electric hand bikes have a motor which assists the user in propelling the wheelchair, making it easier to travel longer distances. Hybrid hand bikes combine both manual and electric features, giving users the option to switch between modes based on their preferences and needs. Each type offers different benefits and caters to varying user preferences and abilities.</p></p>
