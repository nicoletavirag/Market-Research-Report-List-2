<p><p>Lithium Ion Battery Protection ICs market is divided into two main types: Single-cell Protection IC and Multi-cell Protection IC. Single-cell Protection IC is designed for use in applications with only one lithium ion cell, providing basic protection functions such as overcharge, over-discharge, and short circuit protection. On the other hand, Multi-cell Protection IC is used in applications with multiple lithium ion cells connected in series, offering more advanced protection features such as cell balancing and temperature monitoring to ensure the safety and longevity of the battery pack.</p></p>
