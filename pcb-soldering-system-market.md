<p><strong>The PCB Soldering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Oven</li><li>Selective Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB Soldering System market is segmented into three main types: Wave Soldering Ovens, Reflow Ovens, and Selective Ovens. Wave Soldering Ovens utilize a wave of molten solder to solder components onto the PCB. Reflow Ovens use controlled heating and cooling to melt solder paste and create strong connections. Selective Ovens offer precise soldering capabilities for specific areas on the PCB. Each type of soldering system caters to different manufacturing needs and requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
