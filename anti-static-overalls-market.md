<p><p>Anti-static overalls are available in two main types on the market: Split Type and Siamese Type. Split Type overalls consist of two separate pieces - a jacket and pants - that can be worn independently. This design offers more flexibility and customization for the wearer. Siamese Type overalls, on the other hand, are one-piece suits that cover the entire body. These overalls provide full-body protection and are often favored for their simplicity and ease of use. Both types are popular choices in various industries requiring anti-static clothing.</p></p>
