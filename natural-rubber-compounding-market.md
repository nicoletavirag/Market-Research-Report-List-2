<p><ul><li>Solid Natural Rubber</li><li>Concentrated Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural rubber compounding market includes two main types: solid natural rubber and concentrated latex. Solid natural rubber is produced by coagulating the latex obtained from the rubber tree, while concentrated latex is processed by centrifuging the latex to remove water and increase the rubber content. Solid natural rubber is commonly used in the production of various rubber products, while concentrated latex is often preferred in industries where liquid rubber is necessary for the manufacturing process, such as medical gloves and rubber adhesives.</p></p>
