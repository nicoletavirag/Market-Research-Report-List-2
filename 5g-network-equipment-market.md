<p><p>The 5G network equipment market consists of various types such as macro cells, small cells, remote radio units (RRU), active antenna units (AAU), RF filters, baseband units (BBU), phase shifters, and energy supply equipment. Macro cells are large base stations that provide wide coverage, while small cells are used to enhance coverage in densely populated areas. RRUs and AAUs support the transmission of radio signals. RF filters help in signal processing, while BBUs handle baseband processing. Phase shifters adjust the phase of signals, and energy supply equipment powers the network infrastructure.</p></p>
