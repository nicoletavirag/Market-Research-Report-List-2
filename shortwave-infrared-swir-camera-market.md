<p><p>Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) cameras are used for imaging in the near-infrared spectrum from 0.9 to 1.7 microns. There are two main types of SWIR cameras: SWIR area cameras and SWIR linear cameras. </p><p>SWIR area cameras capture images in a two-dimensional field of view, making them suitable for applications like surveillance, industrial inspection, and scientific research. </p><p>SWIR linear cameras capture images in a linear fashion, ideal for tasks such as spectroscopy, line-scan imaging, and sorting applications. Both types cater to a range of industries requiring sensitive and high-resolution imaging capabilities in the SWIR spectrum.</p></p>
