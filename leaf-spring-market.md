<p><p>Leaf springs are suspension components commonly used in vehicles to absorb shock and support the weight of the vehicle. There are two main types of leaf springs in the market: Multi-Leaf Spring and Mono-Leaf Spring. Multi-Leaf Springs consist of multiple layers of spring steel stacked on top of each other, providing more cushioning and stability. On the other hand, Mono-Leaf Springs are made of a single curved piece of spring steel, offering a simpler design with lighter weight. Both types have their own benefits and are used in various vehicles depending on their specific suspension needs.</p></p>
