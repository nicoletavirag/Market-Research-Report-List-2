<p><strong>The Industrial Tire (IC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Tire (IC) market is divided into two main types: Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias tires are constructed with multiple rubber layers overlapping at angles, providing strength and durability in industrial applications. Radial tires have layers of cord arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, giving them better stability and traction. While Bias tires are cost-effective and offer good load carrying capacity, Radial tires provide better fuel efficiency and longer tread life in heavy-duty industrial settings.</p></p>
