<p><p>Fingerprint scanners are divided into two main categories: single finger scanners and four-finger scanners. Single finger scanners are designed to scan and authenticate one fingerprint at a time, making them suitable for individual use in devices like smartphones and laptops. On the other hand, four-finger scanners can capture and compare four fingerprints simultaneously, making them ideal for applications where higher security and accuracy are required, such as border control and law enforcement. Both types of scanners play a crucial role in enhancing security and access control measures.</p></p>
