<p><p>Mineral-based backer boards are high-density panels made from a mixture of Portland cement, ground sand, and reinforced fibers. They are commonly used in construction for tiling applications, providing a stable and durable surface for tile installations in areas such as bathrooms, kitchens, and outdoor patios. These backer boards offer several advantages, including resistance to moisture, mold, and mildew, as well as providing a solid base for tile adhesion.</p><p>The mineral-based backer board market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for construction materials that offer durability and longevity.
