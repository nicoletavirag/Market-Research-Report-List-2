<p><p>Surgical cautery pencils are commonly classified into two types based on their control mechanisms - hand control pencils and foot control pencils. Hand control pencils require manual activation by the surgeon, providing them with immediate control over the surgical process. On the other hand, foot control pencils are operated through a foot pedal, allowing the surgeon to have both hands free for other tasks during the procedure. Both types of pencils offer different levels of convenience and control for surgeons during surgical procedures.</p></p>
