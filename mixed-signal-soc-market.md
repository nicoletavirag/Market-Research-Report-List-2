<p><p>The mixed signal SoC market can be categorized into standard cell-based and embedded design-based. Standard cell-based designs involve using pre-designed building blocks to create a custom SoC tailored to specific requirements. Embedded design-based market, on the other hand, involves incorporating mixed signal functionality into a pre-designed system-on-chip platform. While standard cell-based designs offer more flexibility and customization options, embedded design-based solutions provide a quicker time-to-market and lower development costs. Both approaches play a key role in meeting the diverse needs of the mixed signal SoC market.</p></p>
