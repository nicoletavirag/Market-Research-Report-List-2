<p><p>Electroactive Polymers are a type of smart material that can change their shape or size when an electric field is applied to them. There are two main types of Electroactive Polymers - Electronic Type and Ionic Type. Electronic Type Electroactive Polymers are characterized by their ability to conduct electricity, making them suitable for applications such as sensors and actuators. On the other hand, Ionic Type Electroactive Polymers respond to the movement of ions within the material, which can be used in applications such as artificial muscles.</p></p>
