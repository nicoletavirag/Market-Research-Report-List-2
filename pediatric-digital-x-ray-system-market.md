<p><p>The Pediatric Digital X-ray System market is divided into two types: CR Tech Digital X-Ray System and DR Tech Digital X-Ray System. CR Tech systems use computed radiography technology, which involves scanning imaging plates to produce digital images. On the other hand, DR Tech systems utilize direct radiography technology, where images are captured digitally without the need for a physical imaging plate. Both types offer high-quality images for pediatric patients while minimizing radiation exposure, making them popular choices in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
