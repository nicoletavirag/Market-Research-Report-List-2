<p><strong>The Automotive TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) market consists of two main types: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS devices only conduct in one direction, and are commonly used for protecting circuits from voltage spikes. On the other hand, Bi-polar TVS devices can conduct in both directions, providing protection from both positive and negative voltage spikes. Both types of TVS devices play a crucial role in safeguarding automotive electronics from transient voltage events, ensuring the smooth operation and longevity of automotive systems.</p></p>
