<p><p>Energy (UV/EB) curable resins market consists of two main types: Ultraviolet (UV) Curing and Electron beam (EB) Curing. UV curing utilizes ultraviolet light to initiate a rapid polymerization process, creating a durable and high-performance surface. On the other hand, EB curing employs high-energy electrons to initiate polymerization, resulting in a reliable and efficient curing process. Both technologies offer fast curing times, improved performance characteristics, and environmental benefits, making them increasingly popular in various industries including coatings, adhesives, and printing.</p></p>
