<p><p>High-purity and ultra-pure synthetic quartz glass are advanced materials used in various industries for their exceptional purity and performance characteristics. Two common market types for these materials are Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Plasma Deposition. CVD involves growing a quartz layer on a substrate by the chemical reaction of gas-phase precursors, while Plasma Deposition uses plasma to deposit quartz on a surface. Both methods ensure the production of high-quality quartz glass with minimal impurities, making them ideal for specialized applications.</p></p>
