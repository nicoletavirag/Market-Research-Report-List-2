<p><p>Dietary fiber market can be categorized into two main types: Soluble Dietary Fiber and Insoluble Dietary Fiber. Soluble fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance in the digestive system, which can help lower cholesterol and regulate blood sugar levels. Insoluble fiber does not dissolve in water and adds bulk to the stool, promoting regular bowel movements and preventing constipation. Both types of fiber are essential for maintaining a healthy digestive system and overall well-being.</p></p>
