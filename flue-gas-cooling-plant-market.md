<p><strong>The Flue Gas Cooling Plant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Cooling</li><li>Indirect Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flue Gas Cooling Plants can be categorized into two main types: Direct Cooling and Indirect Cooling. Direct Cooling involves bringing flue gas into direct contact with a cooling medium to lower its temperature, while Indirect Cooling uses a heat exchanger to cool the flue gas without direct contact. Direct Cooling is typically more energy efficient but requires more maintenance, while Indirect Cooling is less energy-intensive but may have higher initial costs. Both types of cooling methods are commonly used in industrial applications to reduce emissions and improve process efficiency.</p></p>
