<p><p>Halogen-free flame retardant adhesive is available in two main types: silicone-rubber base and polyester resin base. Silicone-rubber base adhesive is known for its high temperature resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for applications where extreme heat or movement is a concern. On the other hand, polyester resin base adhesive offers good adhesion properties and durability, making it suitable for various bonding applications. Both types of adhesives provide flame retardant properties without the use of halogen additives, making them environmentally friendly options for flame retardant adhesives.</p></p>
