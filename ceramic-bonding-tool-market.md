<p><strong>The Ceramic Bonding Tool Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillaries</li><li>Wedge Bonding Tools</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic bonding tools are essential in the semiconductor industry for creating reliable connections between semiconductor components. Capillaries are widely used for delicate and precise bonding applications, while wedge bonding tools are favored for thick wire bonding and high-power applications. Capillaries provide a smaller contact area, leading to finer and more accurate bonds, whereas wedge bonding tools provide a larger contact area for stronger connections. Both types of bonding tools play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and high-quality semiconductor bonding processes.</p></p>
