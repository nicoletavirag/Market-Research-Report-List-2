<p><p>Single Sided FPC Coverlay is a type of flexible printed circuit (FPC) material that is used to cover the conductive traces on one side of the FPC. It provides protection against external elements such as moisture and dust, as well as mechanical support to the circuit. Single Sided FPC Coverlay is typically made of a flexible polymer material like polyimide, which is lightweight, durable, and highly flexible.</p><p>The Single Sided FPC Coverlay Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period.
