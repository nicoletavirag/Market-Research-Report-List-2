Wheel Motor Electric Motor Transaxle: The motor is integrated into the wheel hub, providing individual power to each wheel.</p><p>2. Central Motor Electric Motor Transaxle: The motor is located at the center of the vehicle, providing power to the drive wheels through a transmission system.</p><p>3. In-Wheel Motor Electric Motor Transaxle: The motor is housed within the wheel itself, eliminating the need for a separate transmission system.</p></p>
