<p><p>The Purge Control Solenoid Valve market consists of three main types: 2-way valves, 3-way valves, and 4-way valves. 2-way valves control the flow of fluid in a straight path, making them suitable for simple on/off applications. 3-way valves divert the flow of fluid between two outlets, allowing for more versatile control. 4-way valves regulate the flow of fluid between two inlet and two outlet ports, enabling complex fluid control systems. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
