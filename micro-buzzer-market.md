<p><ul><li>Mechanical Buzzer</li><li>Electromechanical Buzzer</li><li>Piezoelectric Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro buzzers are small, lightweight devices used for producing sound alerts in various electronic devices. There are three main types of micro buzzers found in the market: Mechanical buzzers, Electromechanical buzzers, and Piezoelectric buzzers. Mechanical buzzers use a vibrating reed or diaphragm to produce sound. Electromechanical buzzers combine mechanical components with electromagnets to create sound. Piezoelectric buzzers generate sound by using the piezoelectric effect of certain materials. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications in different electronic devices.</p></p>
