<p><p>High Purity SiC Powder Market consists of two primary types - β-SiC and α-SiC. β-SiC is a hexagonal crystal structure that is thermodynamically stable at high temperatures, making it suitable for use in high-temperature applications such as electronics and ceramics. On the other hand, α-SiC has a cubic crystal structure and is often utilized in abrasive and cutting tools due to its superior hardness and wear resistance. Both types of SiC powders play a crucial role in various industrial applications requiring high purity materials.</p></p>
