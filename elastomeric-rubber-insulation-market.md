<p><p>Elastomeric rubber insulation can be categorized into different types based on the material used. NBR based insulation is known for its high resistance to oil and fuel, EPDM based insulation offers excellent weathering and UV resistance, and Chloroprene based insulation provides good resistance to weathering and chemicals.
