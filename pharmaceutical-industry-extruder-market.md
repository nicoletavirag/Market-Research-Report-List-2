<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry extruder market is primarily divided into two main types: single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders are simpler in design and are better suited for processing materials that are easy to work with, such as powders or granules. On the other hand, twin screw extruders are more complex and versatile, making them ideal for handling a wider range of materials with varying viscosities, temperatures, and consistencies in pharmaceutical production processes.</p></p>
