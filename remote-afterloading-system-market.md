<p><p>Remote afterloading system market consists of two main types - HDR (High Dose Rate) afterloaders and PDR (Pulsed Dose Rate) afterloaders. HDR afterloaders deliver a precise and high dose of radiation in a short amount of time, making them ideal for certain cancer treatments. PDR afterloaders deliver radiation in pulses, allowing for a combination of high and low doses over a longer period. Both types of afterloaders offer advanced technology for precise and effective cancer treatment.</p></p>
